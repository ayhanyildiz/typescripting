## Emphasis
<!-- tabs:start -->
#### ** Bold / Italic / Strikethrough **
1. `*This is italic*`
1. `_This is also italic_`
1. `**This is bold**`
1. `__This is also bold__`
1. `**_This this is bold and italic_**`
1. `***This this is bold and italic***`
1. `___This this is bold and italic___`
1. `~~This will be Strikethrough~~`

1. *This is italic*
